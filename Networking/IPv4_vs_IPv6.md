# Networking Discovery: IP Versions
## Date: 2026-03-24

### What I learned:
1. My ISP uses both IPv4 and IPv6
2. `curl ifconfig.me` showed my **IPv6** address (long hexadecimal string).
3. `curl -4 ifconfig.me` showed my **IPv4** address (standard decimal format).

### Why it matters:
IPv6 is the future because we ran out of IPv4 addresses. IPv6 allows for almost infinite device connections.
